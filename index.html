Any contribution is welcome in any form. Contact us to find out how you can help: We need hands to help cook meals, people to help serve the meals on the weekends, drivers to collect the food and other donations for the shelters and of course monetary donations keep the foundation running.

Weekly food and blanket Drive

We have started with Culemborg shelter and had a huge success in conjunction with project helping hands. We have also identified several other shelters in need that we are wanting to expand into depending on the output of food and blankets that we can accumulate during the week.

How we started

Easter Sunday 2020

What initially started as a thought to try and help the homeless closest to our area turned into a major initiative to try and help as many people as we could.

Our community members in need have always relied on shelters to provide a safe warm space and often their only warm meal for the day. We wondered how places like the Culenborg shelter were managing during the lockdown because we know they rely heavily on support from donations and other community members to keep the shelter running.

With the help of Pat Pillay and Project Helping Hands we managed to find a space to cook and distribute food as well as a contact to find more shelters in need. Our first week we managed to provide food for over 300 people as well as some blankets and clothes for the shelter. This was met with a fantastic show of support from social media and the following week we were able to feed 1000 people. Hopefully we can keep this support growing and extend our reach further.
